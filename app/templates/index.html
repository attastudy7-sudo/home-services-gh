Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about GhanaServe.

Post a job describing what you need and your area in Accra. Verified professionals respond with quotes. You compare them, accept the best one, and pay via MoMo or card once the job is done.

Yes — posting a job on GhanaServe is completely free for customers. You only pay the professional directly after the work is completed to your satisfaction.

Every professional on GhanaServe submits their Ghana Card and relevant trade certificates during registration. Our team reviews and approves each profile before they can receive leads or submit quotes. Customers also leave ratings after every completed job.

GhanaServe supports Mobile Money payments from MTN, Telecel, and AirtelTigo, as well as card payments. Cash payment can also be arranged directly with your professional.

GhanaServe covers areas across Accra (including East Legon, Osu, Labone, Cantonments, Airport Residential, Spintex, Tema, Madina, Dansoman, Haatso, Achimota, and more) and Kumasi (including Bantama, Adum, Suame, Asokwa, Manhyia, Oforikrom, and more).
